The Studio

Why we build

Balkan Byte was born from a simple belief: this region has one of the richest, most untouched veins of mythology, history, and storytelling left in gaming.

We're an independent studio based in Plovdiv — one of Europe's oldest cities — building games that feel different because they are different.

No filler. No bloat. Just craft, intention, and a region the world hasn't fully discovered yet.
